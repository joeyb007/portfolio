## What drives me

I'm genuinely curious about how things work at a deep level. I build things to understand them — not just to ship them. I care about making powerful technology accessible to people who wouldn't normally have access to it. Scholr came from watching people without research backgrounds struggle to extract knowledge from scientific papers. PantryPal came from my own frustration with LLMs hallucinating recipes that broke my diet.

I think AI is the most important technological shift of my lifetime, and I want to be someone who builds on top of it — not just someone who uses it.
